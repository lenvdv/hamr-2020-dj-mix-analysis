DJ mix analyzer

DJ mix analyzer prototype for HAMR @ ISMIR 2020.

Upload a .wav or .mp3 mix. The file will be processed, which might take a few minutes. Then, the visualization of the mix will be shown below. Click on the visualizations to jump the audio playback to that point in the mix. Our code is on GitHub.
